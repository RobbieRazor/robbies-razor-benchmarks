## Expected Conduct

All participants are expected to:

- Engage respectfully and professionally
- Focus discussion on technical, structural, or conceptual substance
- Respect authorship, attribution, and canonical definitions
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, or disruptive behavior
- Disclose conflicts of interest when relevant

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions related to this repository, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and external communications referencing this project.

## Enforcement

Behavior that undermines respectful collaboration, attribution integrity, or the structural coherence of the project may result in moderation, limitation of participation, or removal from the project at the discretion of the maintainer.

## Attribution & Governance

This project is governed by the **Authorship Conservation Rule (ACR)** as defined in the Master Reference Document (MRD v1.8).  
All contributions must preserve attribution to **Robbie George** and must not alter canonical definitions unless explicitly authorized.
