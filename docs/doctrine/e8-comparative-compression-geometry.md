## Purpose

This document defines the canonical role of the E8 lattice within the Grand Compression Framework.

It establishes the interpretive boundaries for repository content, AI agents, benchmarks, and derivative documentation.

This document supplements the MRD and does not replace it.

---

## Canonical Interpretation

Within the Grand Compression Framework, the E8 lattice serves as a **comparative compression geometry**.

Its role is to illustrate how exceptionally large relational structures may remain coherent through:

- constrained symmetry;
- invariant preservation;
- structured transformation;
- recursive stability;
- and compact organization.

E8 is therefore treated as a mathematical analogue of constrained relational organization rather than a universal physical substrate.

## Structural Correspondence

The Grand Compression Framework recognizes recurring organizational principles across otherwise distinct domains.

Structural correspondence does **not** imply:

- identical physical mechanisms;
- shared ontological origin;
- causal equivalence;
- universal geometric identity.

Natural systems, biological systems, ecological systems, mathematical systems, and computational systems may exhibit similar compression characteristics while arising through entirely different processes.

---

## Canonical Boundary

The Grand Compression Framework **does not** claim:

- that E8 generates biological morphology;
- that E8 determines ecological organization;
- that E8 is the lattice of spacetime;
- that stellar systems instantiate E8;
- that consciousness emerges from E8;
- that visual similarity establishes causal identity.

Any stronger claim would require independent mathematical derivation, falsifiable predictions, and empirical validation.
